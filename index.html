Welcome to PlumX

The fastest way to make writing a collaborative experience

PlumX makes it easy for teachers to create opportunities for students to fall in
love with writing — in just a few clicks, add collaborative projects, or track
students’ progress through automatically-generated reports. Together, we give
students a great time writing.


How PlumX Works



Create writing assignments

Assign writing projects to classes
with your own custom questions. Get
students to work independently, or
quickly create groups of students for
collaborative projects. Add custom
Reminders to help students focus
their writing practice, or pick from the
list our educators put together.

Get insights on students’
writing progress

Quickly see how a student has been
improving over their assignments
on the Student Progress Report. This
feature gives an overview of a student’s
grasp of grammar and their vocabulary use,
so you can better support the needs
of each student.

Review students’ work

Get updates on students’ working
progress on their assignments.
When a student submits their work,
review it and leave feedback on their
overall work, as well as specific
comments on a part of the text.




Invite students to online
classes seamlessly

Need to cover a topic in greater detail?
Schedule an online class on
PlumGrove. Students get a
notification immediately, and they can
join the session all from the
PlumX portal.
